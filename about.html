About this app

Tacho Quiz was created by a professional driver who understands first-hand how challenging the early stages of a driving career can be. When I first entered the industry, I found the learning process overwhelming - especially when it came to understanding driving times, rest periods, tachograph rules, and preparing for the CPC qualification.

This application was built with a simple purpose: to make learning easier, clearer, and more accessible for drivers at any stage of their journey. Tacho Quiz helps drivers revise essential knowledge through short, focused questions that can be completed anywhere - during breaks, at home, or while preparing for CPC assessments.

The quiz format encourages repetition and confidence-building, allowing users to strengthen their understanding of key regulations without pressure. Whether you are a new driver navigating tachograph rules or an experienced professional refreshing your knowledge, this app supports you in becoming safer, more informed, and better prepared.

Tacho Quiz was created by a driver, for drivers - with the goal of making the path toward CPC certification and professional development smoother and more approachable.
